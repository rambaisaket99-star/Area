Area ✦ — Conversational AI by SuthYank

Area is a client-side conversational agent built on deterministic pattern-matching with stochastic response selection. It operates on a finite-state affective model, adapting to user sentiment and conversation history.

Core Features:
  • Gender-Adaptive Engine: Detects gender signals and adjusts tone.
  • Multi-Domain Responses: Gaming, STEM, social media, wellness, tech, relationships, and more.
  • Persistent Sessions: Chats saved locally on your device.
  • Repetition Detection: Detects and handles repeated messages.
  • Smart Quiz System: Area asks thought-provoking quizzes anytime!
  • 11 Light Themes: Aqua, Jade, Teal, Sky, Cerulean, Periwinkle, and more.
